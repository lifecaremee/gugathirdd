Key Components

    Absorption of Mycotxins:
    • specially formulated with high-affinity binders like activated clay, yeast cell wall components, and organic acids, Merlin Fix selectively absorbs a wide range of mycotoxins (such as aflatoxins, ochratoxins, fumonisins) in the gastrointestinal tract.
      • Prevention of Absorption:
    • by binding toxins firmly, Merlin Fix prevents them from crossing the intestinal barrier, thereby reducing their bioavailability and toxicity.
      • Detoxification & Biotransformation:
    • some componcnts of Merlin Fix (such as enzymes and beneficial microbes) may biologically degrade certain mycotoxins into non-toxic metabolites, further reducing the toxic load.
      • Protection of Gut Integrity:
    • helps maintain gut health by reducing inflammation and oxidative stress caused by mycotoxins, improving nutrient absorption and immunity
      • Liver Support:
    • Specific ingredients in Merlin Fix support liver function and acts as a powerfulantioxidant, aiding in the detoxifaction process and neutralizing harmful toxins in the body.
