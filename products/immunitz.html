Key Components
  • LYSOZYMES: Lysozymes are mainly involved to maintain specific immunity by penetrating in the microbial cell wall's outer (peptidoglycan) layer, covert them in small fragments. These fragments of the bacteria are then presented to B and T cells for making antibodies for bird immunity.
  • NUCLEOTIDES: Nucleotides increase the number of immune cells like leukocytes and macrophages. They also contribute to the production of immunoglobulins such as IgA, IgG and IgM which are vital for fighting off infections.
  • OMEGA 3: It is added from fish oil and increases B-cells and Re T cells production in blood; to increase adaptive and non-adaptive immune response. Neutrophils
  • VITAMIN C: Vit C has specific role in production of B and T cells macrophages and dendritic cells. It plays an important role in production of multiple immune synthetic cells reactions. It has a role in phagocytosis of the foreign pathogenic cells by increasing the neutrophils.
  • GARLIC: It has strong anti-oxidant capability and prevents cell from tissue damage. ANILLINE Ca, K and S are main active ingredients in it which have strong antibacterial, antiviral and antifungal activity.
  • ECHINACEA: It has specific role in increasing the phagocytic activity of the macrophages by increasing cytokine levels and thus it produces specific and non-specific antibodies. It contains alkamides/caffeic, flavonoids and glycoproteins for production of anti-bodies for better immune status.
  • SELENIUM: By controlling oxidative stress, selenium helps to maintain cellular integrity and proper function, which is crucial for the effective operation of the immune system.
