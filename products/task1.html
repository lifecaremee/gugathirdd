INDICATIONS:

    • TASK one is an ideal hepatoprotective and hepatic regenerative liver conditioner. Its usage in poultry includes sudden increased mortality, lethargy, ruffled feathers, pale combs, a sharp drop in egg production, and abdominal distension. A post-mortem examination can reveal the liver as enlarged, pale, yellow, soft, and friable, sometimes with hemorrhage and excess fat.

    BENEFITS:

    • lt is supplemented to support liver in detoxification and matures the structural and functional integrity of liver cells.
    • lt stimulates sluggish liver parenchyma cells and restores liver functions.

    • It protects the liver from being enlarged, pale, yellow, soft, and friable.
    • lt also help liver with hemorrhage free and removes excess fat and converts in metabolized energy.
