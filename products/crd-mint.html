CRD Mint

Poultry

Composition

Isopropyl Alcohol (USP) 10%
Menthol (USP) 6.7%
Eucalyptus Oil (USP) 7%
Peppermint Oil (USP) 15%
Pine Oil (USP) 1%
Thyme Oil (USP) 1.5%
Glycerin (USP) 5%

Ingredients

    Eucalyptus Oil
    • Has antibacterial and antifungal effects on pathogenic bacteria in the respiratory tract
    • Has anti-inflammatory properties
    • Inhaled Eucalyptus oil vapors act as decongestants
    • Stimulates immune system
      • Menthol
    • Natural decongestant that helps in breaking up mucus and treats swollen or congested sinuses
    • Has a pleasant and refreshing aroma
    • Has antibacterial properties to inhibit bacterial growth
      • Peppermint Oil
    • Helps to clear the respiratory tract
    • Provides instantaneous relief from nasal
      • congestion and sinusitis
    • Stimulates digestion
    • Helps to relieve stress in poultry
      • Pine Oil:
    • Helps to reduce the inflammation of upper and lower respiratory tract swelling
    • Helps to improve air quality
    • Has antibacterial, antiviral, and antifungal effect
    • Helps to reduce muscles pain
    • Stimulates the immune system
      • Thyme Oil:
    • Improves feed conversion rate (FCR) and decrease liver enzyme leakage, indicating better tolerance to high temperatures.
    • Increases immunoglobulin A (IgA) levels in poultry, enhancing their immune response.
      • Glycerin:
    • Lubricating and softening actions in respiratory tract
    • Effective in wound healing
    • Energy source in broiler metabolized by liver
    • Long term preservative of product
    • Anti-bacterial, Anti-viral and anti-inflammatory properties

    Packaging

    1 Liter, 5 Liters

    • Drinking Water: 1ml per 5 Liters for 4–5 days
    • Spraying: 20ml per 1 Liter
    • Fogging: 20ml per 1 Liter
